# ✨ TejAstra - Your Minimal Path to Focus

**TejAstra** is a simple Android launcher designed to help you break phone addiction and get your time back. It turns your smartphone into a "focused tool" rather than a "distraction machine."

---

## 🚀 Why TejAstra?
We use our phones too much. Apps like Instagram and YouTube are designed to keep us scrolling. **TejAstra** changes that by making your phone boring for distractions and great for productivity.

## 🌟 Key Features (The Easy Version)

### 📱 1. Boring (Minimalist) Home Screen
- No colorful icons that beg to be clicked.
- Only show the apps you *really* need.
- Clean and dark design to stay calm.

### 🧘 2. The "Breathe" Delay
- When you try to open a "bad" app (like Social Media), the phone makes you wait for 5 seconds and take a breath.
- This short pause helps you decide if you *really* need to use the app or if it's just a habit.

### ⏲️ 3. Deep Work Timer
- A built-in timer for your work sessions.
- Helps you focus on one thing for 25 minutes (Pomodoro style).

### 📵 4. Dumb-Phone Mode & Focus Modes
- Turn your smart phone into a simple "dumb phone."
- Create different "Focus Modes" like *Work Mode*, *Study Mode*, or *Sleep Mode* where only specific apps are allowed.

### 📊 5. Screen Time Tracking
- See exactly where your time is going. 
- Understand your habits so you can change them.

### 💡 6. Daily Motivation
- A custom tagline or quote on your home screen to remind you why you're staying focused.

---

## 🛠️ How to use it?
1. **Download and Install**: Open the app on your Android phone.
2. **Set as Default Launcher**: Go to your phone settings and choose **TejAstra** as your Home App.
3. **Pick your Apps**: Choose only 4 or 5 apps that you want to see on your home screen.
4. **Enable Focus Service**: Turn on the accessibility service so TejAstra can help you stay away from distracting apps.

---

## 🧩 Tech Stuff (For Nerds 🤓)
- Built with **Kotlin** and **Android Jetpack Compose**.
- Uses **Accessibility Services** to block apps.
- Uses **Usage Stats** to track screen time.
